## What FleetPulse Is (bullet summary)

- **Product:** FleetPulse is a **fleet management software platform** (web app). Tagline: *Modern Fleet Management*.
- **One-liner:** Track vehicles, drivers, maintenance, inspections, and documents in one place. Built for fleet managers and operations teams.
- **Who built it:** Prush Logistics Group LLC is the company behind FleetPulse (you are the CEO). FleetPulse is the product; Prush Logistics is the delivery/logistics company that also offers this software.

### Core features

- **Vehicle tracking** – Mileage, oil change due mileage, maintenance schedules, vehicle status (active, out of service, in shop).
- **Driver management** – Assign drivers to vehicles, track assignments, driver info and compliance.
- **Digital inspections** – Pre-trip and post-trip inspections with photo documentation and sign-offs.
- **Service records** – Full service history with costs and provider info.
- **Issue tracking** – Report and track vehicle issues with priority levels and status updates.
- **Document management** – Store and track documents (e.g. registration, insurance) with expiration reminders.
- **Fleet health dashboard** – Overview with oil change status (OK / due soon / overdue), inspection pass rates, open issues, expired documents, vehicle counts by status.
- **CSV import** – Bulk import vehicles from CSV for quick setup.
- **Mobile-friendly** – Responsive so it works on phones and tablets in the field.
- **Custom dashboards** – Templates: Default, Compact, Executive; plus custom layouts (sections, tabs) via Control Panel / Template Builder.
- **Multi-company** – Companies can have multiple users; company switcher for agencies or groups with more than one fleet.

### Where it lives

- **Product site:** FleetPulseHQ.com (landing, login, signup, feature list, contact).
- **Contact:** fleetpulse@fastmail.com.

### Positioning for Prush Logistics

- FleetPulse is **our** platform—we use it and we offer it to other fleets.
- Prush Logistics = delivery driver company + the company that builds/operates FleetPulse.
- Good angles for PrushLogistics.com: “We run our own fleet on FleetPulse” / “We built FleetPulse to run fleets like ours” / “Fleet management software by a logistics company that gets it.”
