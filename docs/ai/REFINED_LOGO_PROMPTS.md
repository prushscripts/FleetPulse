## 📝 Key Differences to Emphasize in Prompts

When generating logos, make sure to specify:
- ✅ **"Commercial truck"** or **"delivery van"** or **"box truck"**
- ✅ **"NOT a minivan"** or **"NOT an SUV"**
- ✅ **"Pulse line extending FROM the vehicle"** (not cramped inside)
- ✅ **"Bold, prominent pulse/heartbeat line"**
- ✅ **"Professional fleet vehicle"**
